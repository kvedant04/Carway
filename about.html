About Us

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help the site remember information about your visit.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our site
  • Deliver personalized content
  • Improve our services

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember choices you make
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What are your business hours?
A: Our dealerships are open Monday-Friday from 9AM-7PM and Saturday from 9AM-5PM.

Q: Do you offer financing options?
A: Yes, we work with multiple lenders to help you find the best financing options for your needs.

Vehicle Questions

Q: Are all vehicles certified?
A: All our new vehicles come with full manufacturer certification. Used vehicles undergo a rigorous 150-point inspection.

Q: Do you offer test drives?
A: Yes, we encourage test drives for any vehicle you're interested in. Just bring your valid driver's license.

Service Questions

Q: Do you service vehicles not purchased from CarWay?
A: Yes, our service centers are open to all vehicles regardless of where they were purchased.

Q: How often should I get my vehicle serviced?
A: We recommend following the manufacturer's maintenance schedule, typically every 5,000-7,500 miles.
